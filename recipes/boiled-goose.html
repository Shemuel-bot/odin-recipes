Boiled Goose

Description

This is my favorite boiled goose recipe its simple, quick, and easy to make. It also has a hilarious story to it, its but I'm not going to bother about nazi germany today. Let's just get right into the ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Goose
  • Milk
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Sage Leaves

Steps:

  1. Prep your goose
  2. Pour milk atop it and let it sit over night
  3. Dry well with a towel
  4. Add salt and Pepper
  5. Chop and add onions and add sage Leaves
  6. Hang it by the neck over the course of the day
  7. Boil for 1 hour
